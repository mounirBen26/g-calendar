Privacy Policy

This extension respects your privacy. Here's how we handle your data:

  • Data Collection: The extension accesses your Google Calendar to retrieve calendar events and display them. No additional data is collected.
  • Data Usage: Your data is used locally on your device to show events. It is not sent to any external servers.
  • Data Storage: No data is stored permanently. All access is temporary during the session.
  • Your Rights: You can revoke the extension's access via your Google account settings at any time.
